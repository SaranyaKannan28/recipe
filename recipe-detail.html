Chocolate Cake

A delicious chocolate cake recipe that is easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes.
